Hang Man

					
1. Display a hanging post

2. Player 1 inputs a word

3. # of lines generate based on the input word length

4. Player 2 inputs a guess letter

	a. If input is in chosen word

		i. Display the letter in the lines in the position it is in in the word

	b. Else: not in the chosen word:

		i. Display a new body part on the hanging post

5. If the hanging post has a complete man on it:

	a. Player 2 loses

6. If the word is guessed before there is a complete man on the hang post:

	a. Player 2 wins
